Hi Albee Account & Data Deletion

Hi Albee gives users full control over their personal data. You may delete your entire account or remove specific data directly within the app at any time.

Delete Your Entire Account

Deleting your account permanently removes all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Hi Albee app
  2. Go to Profile Settings
  3. Tap “Delete Account and All Data”
  4. Confirm your deletion request

What Is Deleted

  • Account information (including email and user ID)
  • Chat history and reflections
  • Insights and reports
  • Enneagram responses
  • Preference and familiarity data

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Delete Individual Data (Without Deleting Your Account)

You may also delete specific data while keeping your account active.

  • Individual chat conversations can be deleted within the chat interface.
  • Selected stored data can be removed through the “My Data” section in Profile Settings.

Deleting individual data removes it from active user storage and prevents it from being used in future insights or functionality.

Data Retention

Some limited technical logs (such as anonymized server logs or crash logs) may be retained temporarily for security, fraud prevention, and system integrity purposes. These logs are automatically deleted within a limited retention period and are not used for advertising or tracking.
